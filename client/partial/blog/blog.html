Sentiment analysis

Sentiment Analysis of a topic and its related sub topics. We track the sentiment as it is expressed in different regions of the world. It can help you make sense of your brand and how it is performing vs other competitors and the over all market around the world.

This working prototype is displaying an analysis graph of about 0.5 million data points.

Green is for positive sentiment. Red is for negative sentiment.

Anything in between is an interpolation of sentiment values between green and red.

Circle size is directly proportional to the amount of data we have in that region. Hovering over the circle will give you the data size and the calculated sentiment value for that circle.

On page load you see over all sentiment of the topic. You can filter down sentiment by keywords inside that topic using the navigation bar.

Zoom in and out on the maps to get a better picture of the sentiment values in a region.
