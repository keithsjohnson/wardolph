About Me

This is an opensource project. Helps make sence of sentiment/emotion associated with a topic in different regions of the world.

Green colour is for positive sentiment.

Red is for negative sentiment.

Anything in between is an interpolation of sentiment values between green and red

On page load you see over all sentiment of the topic. You can filter down sentiment by keywords inside that topic by using the navigation bar.

Zoom in and out on the maps to get a better picture of the sentiment values in a region.
